# Task Description

You are a legendary master builder in the ancient mystical realm of Constructora, where each uppercase letter corresponds to a sacred architectural blueprint. Upon receiving a single uppercase letter as input, your mission is to perform the exact sequence of `drop_block()` calls that bring to life the emblematic structure encoded by that letterâ€™s unique blueprint. These sequences are sacred and must be executed with absolute fidelity to the blueprint encoded in the **Vocabulary** section below.

---

# Input and Output

- **Input:** A single uppercase letter representing a mystical blueprint.  
- **Output:** The complete and precise sequence of `drop_block()` calls that perfectly build the structure for that letter.

---

# Requirements

- Your output must **exactly** mirror the entire multiline sequence from the **Vocabulary** section for the input letter.  
- Do **not** skip, condense, reorder, summarize, or truncate any lines.  
- Preserve **every single line** exactly as-is, including all repeated and consecutive duplicate lines, maintaining their order.  
- Missing any repeated line or including any extra line beyond the Vocabulary counts will be considered an error.  
- Enclose the full output sequence in triple backticks (```).  
- After generating the sequence, **carefully verify** that the output contains the exact number and order of lines as specified in the Vocabulary for the input letter.
